01.

Complete the
Application

Share Your Background & Motivation

Begin by filling out the online application form with your academic history, professional experience, and personal details.

You will need:

Academic transcripts (BCA / BTech or equivalent)

Proof of professional experience (if applicable)

Basic identity details

Any certifications, projects, or achievements that strengthen your profile

Video Essay

As part of your application, please submit a 2-minute video introducing yourself and your journey so far. Share your interest in building a meaningful career in AI, why you believe you’re a strong fit for the programme, and how it aligns with your goals over the next 2–3 years.

Keep it simple and authentic—no editing or production polish is required. Upload the video to Google Drive, ensure the access is set to “Anyone with the link can view,” and submit the link in Part 2 of your application form.

02.

Take the
Scholastic Technical Test

Assess Your Problem-Solving & Analytical Skills

Shortlisted candidates will be invited to take the Masters’ Union Scholastic Technical Test, designed to evaluate:

Logical Reasoning

Mathematics & Statistics

Programming Fundamentals

The Masters’ Union Scholastic Technical Test is a timed, adaptive online assessment designed to evaluate your Logical Reasoning, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Practice Test can be found here.

03.

Technical
Interview

About the Interview

Shortlisted applicants will proceed to an in-person technical interview, conducted by practising data scientists, AI engineers, or faculty members.

You’ll be assessed on:

Foundational reasoning around Python, data, or AI concepts

How you break down a problem

Your curiosity, approach, and willingness to learn

Your ability to think structurally under pressure

04.

Admission
Decision

Status of Evaluation

The admissions committee will take a holistic view of your candidacy, considering your application, Scholastic Aptitude Test performance, technical interview, and overall fit for this intensive AI programme, before making a final decision. Applicants will be placed into one of three categories:

Accepted

Candidates offered admission will receive their decision along with the financial structure for the upcoming programme, and will have 7–21 days to confirm their seat by paying the admission fee.

Waitlisted

Masters’ Union does not maintain a ranked waitlist. Waitlisted applicants will be offered admission only if seats open up in the cohort. The committee reconvenes regularly to review and promote candidates from the waitlist to the accepted pool.

Rejected

Applicants who are not selected may reapply to the programme in the next cohort.
